How to Play
👆
Tap an arrow to escape it off the grid — its path to the edge must be clear of all other arrows.
⏱️
Timer challenge — each level has a countdown timer. Clear faster for huge time bonus points!
🔥
Combo system — tap arrows quickly in a row to build up a combo multiplier (up to x10) for massive scores!
↩
Undo button — made a wrong tap? Undo your last move once per turn to recover.
💡
Hints — stuck? Tap hint to highlight a safe arrow. Costs 50 points per use.
💾
Auto-save — progress is saved automatically. Quit anytime and resume exactly where you left off.
🏆
Leaderboard — your best score for every level is saved. Try to beat your own records!
