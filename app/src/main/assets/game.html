How to Play
👆
Tap an arrow to escape it — its entire path to the edge must be clear of other arrows.
⏱️
Timer challenge — clear the grid before time runs out. Faster = bigger score bonus!
🔥
Combo system — tap arrows quickly in a row to build x2, x3... up to x10 combo multiplier!
↩
Undo button — made a wrong tap? Undo your last move once per turn.
💡
Hints — tap to highlight a safe arrow. Costs 50 points per use.
💾
Auto-save — progress saves automatically. Resume any time from where you left off.
🌙
Dark / Light theme — tap the ☀️ / 🌙 button (top right) to switch themes anytime!
